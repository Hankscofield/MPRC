Report a Park Issue

Help us maintain Myanmar's parks by reporting issues you observe. Your reports make a real difference in our communities.

How to Submit a Report

The MPRC reporting system is designed to make it easy for you to report issues and track their resolution. Follow these simple steps:

Quick Reporting Steps

  1. Identify the issue - Note what you observed and where
  2. Document with photos - Take clear pictures showing the problem
  3. Submit your report - Fill out the form with details
  4. Track progress - Monitor the status of your report

Types of Issues You Can Report

Cleanliness Issues

Overflowing trash bins, litter accumulation, graffiti, vandalism, and general cleanliness concerns.

Safety Hazards

Broken equipment, damaged paths, fallen trees, exposed wires, or any condition that could cause injury.

Facility Problems

Broken benches, damaged tables, non-working lights, restroom issues, or drinking fountain problems.

Environmental Concerns

Damaged vegetation, water quality issues, invasive species, or harm to wildlife.

Before You Report

To make your report as effective as possible, please gather the following information:

  • Location: Park name and specific area (use landmarks like "near the main fountain" or "east playground")
  • Description: Clear explanation of what you observed
  • Photos: Images showing the issue from different angles
  • Timing: When you observed the issue
  • Urgency: Assessment of how urgent the issue is
