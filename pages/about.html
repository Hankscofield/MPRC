About Us

Connecting communities with their parks, one report at a time

Our Mission

We believe every park deserves care and every voice deserves to be heard. Our mission is to bridge the gap between citizens and authorities by providing a simple, accessible platform where park issues can be reported instantly and addressed effectively.

Born from a single observation at 8,000 feet, our platform empowers communities across Myanmar to take an active role in preserving and improving their local green spaces. We envision a future where no park issue goes unnoticed, and every community has the tools to protect their shared natural heritage.

Our Journey

From a single observation on a mountain trail to a community-wide movement for park preservation

Discovery & Insight

While hiking at 8,000 ft, a neglected park is found. Realization: many parks across Myanmar face similar issues silently. Core idea emerges ‚Äî create a simple system that lets citizens report problems and authorities receive them instantly.

Initial Concept Development

Brainstorming on how to connect citizens and government without bureaucracy. Decision to build a website that acts as a central public reporting hub. Features outlined: report submission, issue tracking, park database, and community feedback.

Design Drafts & Wireframes

First layout sketches created. User flow planned: open website ‚Üí select park ‚Üí submit report (text + photo). Accessibility and simplicity prioritized for all users.

Website Development Begins

Core structure coded: homepage, report form, and park listings. Database prepared to store reports and park details. Early demo created for team testing.

System Testing & Adjustments

Report submissions tested in real-time. Bugs fixed: missing icons, form validation, mobile responsiveness. Added admin panel for reviewing and organizing reports.

Final Polishing

UI/UX refined and visual elements improved. Final copywriting, icons, images, and animations added. Privacy and responsible reporting guidelines written.

Public Launch

Website officially published and introduced to local communities. First round of reports submitted by early users. Outreach begins to authorities and community leaders.

Community Engagement & Field Testing

Visited multiple parks to test reporting tools on-site. Collected photos, interviews, and feedback from locals. Improvements planned for Version 1.1.
