Our Mission

To empower the citizens of Myanmar to actively participate in the preservation of public spaces by providing an accessible, transparent tool for reporting and resolving park maintenance issues.

Our Vision

To cultivate a nationwide culture of environmental responsibility, ensuring that every public park in Myanmar becomes a clean, safe, and thriving sanctuary for future generations to enjoy.
