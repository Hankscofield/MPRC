Adding Images to Reports

A step-by-step guide to uploading and sharing images using free online hosting platforms

Visual Guide

How to Add Images to Your Report

Use free image hosting services like ImgBB to include photos in your park reports. Follow these simple steps to get a shareable image URL.

Why Include Images?

Photos help our team understand the exact nature and severity of reported issues. A clear image can speed up response times and ensure the right resources are dispatched.

1

Go to ImgBB Website

Open your browser and navigate to imgbb.com. You'll see a clean homepage with an upload button. No account is required for basic uploads!

Tip: Bookmark this site for quick access when submitting future reports.

2

Upload Your Image

Click the "Start Uploading" button or drag and drop your image directly onto the page. Select the photo you took of the park issue from your device.

Tip: For best results, use clear, well-lit photos that clearly show the issue.

3

Copy the Direct Link

After uploading, ImgBB will show you several link options. Look for the "Direct Link" option and click the copy button next to it. This gives you a URL ending in .jpg or .png.

Tip: Make sure to copy the "Direct Link" - not the HTML or BBCode options.

4

Paste in Your Report

Go to the MPRC report form and paste the copied URL into the Image URL field. Your image will now be included in your report for reviewers to see.

Tip: You can add multiple images by repeating steps 1-3 for each photo.

Troubleshooting

  • • Image not showing? Make sure you copied the "Direct Link" that ends in .jpg, .png, or .gif
  • • Upload failed? Check that your image is under 32MB and in a supported format (JPG, PNG, GIF, BMP)
  • • Link expired? Some services delete images after inactivity. Use ImgBB for reliable long-term hosting.

Quick Summary

1 Visit imgbb.com
2 Upload your photo
3 Copy the direct link
4 Paste in your report

That's all it takes! Your images help us respond faster and more effectively to park issues.
