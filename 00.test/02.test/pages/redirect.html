Quick Start Guide

Step 1: Explore the Etiquette Guides

Before you start reporting, familiarize yourself with what constitutes good and bad park behavior. Our comprehensive guides cover everything from cleanliness to wildlife protection.

Step 2: Observe Your Local Park

Next time you visit a park, pay attention to its condition. Are the bins overflowing? Is equipment damaged? Are there safety hazards? These observations will help you make effective reports.

Step 3: Document Issues

When you spot a problem, take clear photos and note the exact location. Good documentation helps us route your report to the right people quickly.

Step 4: Submit and Track

Use our reporting system to submit your observations. You'll receive a tracking number to follow your report's progress through the resolution process.
