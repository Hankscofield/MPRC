About MPRC

Empowering communities to revitalize their public green spaces through collaboration and action.

Our Mission

The Myanmar Park Report Center (MPRC) exists to bridge the gap between community members and the government agencies responsible for maintaining our public green spaces. We believe that every citizen has both the right and the responsibility to contribute to the upkeep of the parks that serve their communities.

Through our platform, we enable residents to report issues, share observations, and actively participate in the improvement of parks throughout Myanmar. By facilitating direct communication between citizens and authorities, we help ensure that problems are identified quickly and addressed efficiently.

Our Vision

We envision a Myanmar where every community has access to clean, safe, and well-maintained public parks. These green spaces are essential to public health, community connection, and quality of life. Our goal is to create a culture of shared stewardship where citizens and government work together to protect and improve these valuable resources.

The Parks We Want

  • Clean and well-maintained facilities
  • Safe environments for children and families
  • Thriving natural ecosystems
  • Welcoming spaces for all community members
  • Responsive maintenance and improvement

What We Do

Facilitate Reporting

We provide an easy-to-use platform for citizens to report park issues, from overflowing trash bins to safety hazards and everything in between.

Connect Communities

We serve as the bridge between park visitors and government agencies, ensuring that citizen concerns reach the right authorities.

Educate Visitors

Through our comprehensive guides and resources, we help park visitors understand proper etiquette and their role in park stewardship.

Track Progress

We provide transparency by tracking reported issues and keeping citizens informed about resolution progress.

Our Values

  • Community First: We believe in the power of collective action and community engagement.
  • Transparency: We operate openly, keeping citizens informed about how their reports are processed.
  • Respect for Nature: We promote environmental stewardship and protection of natural ecosystems.
  • Inclusivity: We work to ensure parks are welcoming and accessible to all community members.
  • Accountability: We hold ourselves and our partners accountable for results.

Our Partners

MPRC works in partnership with various government agencies and community organizations to achieve our mission:

  • Parks and Recreation Departments
  • Public Works Departments
  • Environmental Services
  • Wildlife and Forestry Services
  • Local Community Organizations
  • Environmental NGOs

These partnerships enable us to route citizen reports to the appropriate authorities and ensure timely action on park issues.

Get Involved

There are many ways you can support MPRC's mission:

  • Submit Reports: When you see an issue in a park, report it through our system
  • Practice Good Etiquette: Follow park rules and lead by example
  • Spread the Word: Tell friends and neighbors about MPRC
  • Volunteer: Participate in park cleanup events and community initiatives
  • Provide Feedback: Help us improve our platform and services

Ready to Make a Difference?

Start by exploring our Park Etiquette Guide to learn about responsible park behavior, or visit our Reports page to submit your first report.
