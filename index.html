Our Mission

Revitalizing Green Spaces,
One Community at a Time

MPRC empowers citizens to share reports, ideas, and suggestions for their local parks. With simple, user-friendly tools and a focus on community voices, we help authorities understand real needs and inspire care for the green spaces that bring everyone together.
